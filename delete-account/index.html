What Information Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, profile photo
  • Account Data: Login credentials, preferences, settings
  • User Content: Posts, photos, reviews, comments
  • Messages: All chat conversations
  • Booking Information: Past and future bookings
  • Chef Data: Business profile, specialty dishes, availability (if applicable)
  • Social Connections: Followers, following, likes

Information We May Retain

For legal and business purposes, we may retain some information:

  • Transaction Records: Financial records for tax and legal compliance (up to 7 years)
  • Legal Requirements: Data required by law or for ongoing legal matters
  • Security Logs: Technical logs for fraud prevention and security

This retained information is kept secure and will not be used for any other purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the deletion process take?

Once we verify your identity, your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

What happens to my active bookings?

If you have active bookings, please cancel or complete them before requesting account deletion. Contact the other party (chef or client) to notify them.

Will chefs I've worked with see my reviews?

Your reviews will be deleted along with your account. This means ratings and reviews you've left will be removed.

What if I'm a chef with active clients?

Please ensure all bookings are completed and communicate with your clients before deleting your account. You may want to export important information first.

Can I just deactivate my account instead?

Currently, we only offer permanent deletion. If you'd like a temporary deactivation feature, please let us know in your deletion request email.

Will my payment information be deleted?

We don't store your full payment information. Transaction records may be retained for legal and tax purposes (up to 7 years) but payment card details are not stored by us.
