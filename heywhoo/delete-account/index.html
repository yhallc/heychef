When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days:

  • Social Data: Connections and interactions with other players

For legal and business purposes, we may retain some information:

  • Transaction Records: Purchase history for legal compliance (if applicable)
  • Legal Requirements: Data required by law or for ongoing legal matters
  • Security Logs: Technical logs for fraud prevention and security

This retained information is kept secure and will not be used for any other purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the deletion process take?

Once we verify your identity, your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

What happens to my tokens?

All tokens (daily, earned, and total) will be deleted along with your account and cannot be recovered or transferred.

Will my chat partners see our messages?

Your chat messages will be anonymized and removed from the system. Other users will not be able to view conversations with you.

What happens to my leaderboard rankings?

Your position on all leaderboards (Top Detectives, Most Fooled, Master Deceivers) will be removed.

Can I just deactivate my account instead?

Currently, we only offer permanent deletion. If you'd like a temporary deactivation feature, please let us know in your deletion request email.

Will I still receive emails after deletion?

No, once your account is deleted, you will be removed from all email lists and will not receive any further communications from HeyWhoo.
